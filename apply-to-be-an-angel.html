Apply to be an Angel

A Personal Message from Founder John W. G.

“At Prevention Pulse, we believe that early intervention can save lives. As an Angel, your role is to provide guidance, empathy, and encouragement to those at risk. Every interaction you have, no matter how small, could make a life-changing difference.

This work requires dedication, patience, and a true desire to help. We welcome you to join us in building a world where individuals feel seen, supported, and safe. Together, we transform hope into action and create real impact every day.”

Training & Responsibilities

Upon approval as an Angel, comprehensive training materials will be provided to ensure you are fully prepared to support those in need. These materials will cover best practices in communication, privacy, crisis response, and guidance techniques.

As an Angel, it is essential that you review all training materials thoroughly and adhere to the protocols outlined. Your duties include actively engaging with individuals in a safe, empathetic manner, maintaining confidentiality, and applying the guidance principles you learned. Consistent completion and understanding of training ensures that you are effective, responsible, and ready to make a meaningful impact in the lives of those reaching out for support.

This commitment guarantees that every Angel is equipped to provide compassionate, knowledgeable support while upholding the integrity and mission of Prevention Pulse.
