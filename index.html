Prevention – Guidance Before Harm

Our mission is to provide a safe, anonymous space for individuals who may be considering harmful actions — toward themselves or others — to reach out and connect with trusted family, mentors, professionals, or trained volunteers (“Angels”).

Through early communication and structured guidance, we aim to prevent tragedies, reduce risk, and provide actionable support. Users are empowered to share feelings, seek advice, or alert someone to potential dangers while maintaining complete control over what personal information is shared.

Every interaction prioritizes safety, respect, and constructive intervention, ensuring both protection for users and transparency for the support network so that preventive actions can be informed and timely.

Go to Dashboard

How It Works

Users can send messages anonymously to family, crisis hotlines, or trusted volunteers. Each message creates an opportunity for intervention, guidance, and support before harm occurs.

The platform prioritizes privacy and safety: identities are revealed only if the sender chooses. All communications are monitored for urgent risks, allowing the platform to act responsibly when necessary to prevent serious harm.

Why It Matters

Early intervention saves lives and prevents harm. By encouraging communication and providing structured support, we reduce the likelihood of crises escalating.

We provide educational resources, tips for family and friends, and guidance on supporting someone in distress. Our goal is to create an informed, proactive community that can help identify and address potential risks effectively.
