Prevention – You’re Not Alone

Our mission is to provide a safe, anonymous space for individuals struggling with thoughts of suicide to reach out and connect with loved ones, trained professionals, and supportive peers. We aim to make that first step easier, offering hope and guidance before it’s too late.

Go to Dashboard

How It Works

Users can send messages anonymously to family, crisis hotlines, or trusted volunteers (“Angels”). Each message is an opportunity for someone to intervene, provide support, or simply listen.

Our platform prioritizes privacy and safety, ensuring that no identities are revealed unless the sender chooses to disclose them.

Why It Matters

Early intervention saves lives. By encouraging communication and providing a structured support system, we help prevent crises before they escalate.

We also provide resources, educational content, and guidance for family members and friends to understand how to support someone in need.

If you are in immediate danger, please dial 988 (U.S.)
