Prevention – Guidance Before Harm

Our mission is to provide a safe, anonymous space for individuals who may be considering harmful actions — toward themselves or others — to reach out and connect with trusted family, mentors, professionals, or trained volunteers (“Angels”). Early communication can prevent tragedies and help people make better choices.

Go to Dashboard

How It Works

Users can send messages anonymously to family, crisis hotlines, or trusted volunteers. Each message creates an opportunity for intervention, guidance, and support before harm occurs.

The platform prioritizes privacy and safety: identities are revealed only if the sender chooses.

Why It Matters

Early intervention saves lives and prevents harm. By encouraging communication and providing structured support, we reduce the likelihood of crises escalating.

We also provide educational resources, tips for family and friends, and guidance on how to support someone in distress or at risk of taking harmful actions.

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line. In the U.S., dial 988.
